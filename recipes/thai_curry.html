Thai Coconut Curry

Description

Thai Coconut Curry is a tantalizing and aromatic dish that harmoniously blends the richness of coconut milk with the vibrant flavors of Thai red curry paste and an assortment of colorful vegetables and tofu. Infused with fragrant garlic, ginger, and onions, this curry exudes a perfect balance of sweet, savory, and spicy notes, offering a comforting warmth with every spoonful. The medley of vegetables, simmered in the velvety coconut broth, creates a delightful textural contrast against the tender tofu, while the finishing touch of soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice adds a zesty and tangy punch. Served atop a bed of steaming rice or noodles, this Thai Coconut Curry is a sensory delight that captivates the palate with its exotic flavors and vibrant hues, making it a true culinary adventure for the senses.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snow peas)
  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add diced onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the Thai red curry paste and cook for another minute.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and broth, stirring well to combine. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and tofu cubes to the simmering curry. Cook for about 10-15 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  6. Serve the Thai coconut curry over cooked rice or noodles, garnished with fresh cilantro.
