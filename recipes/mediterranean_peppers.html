Mediterranean Stuffed Bell Peppers

Description

Mediterranean Stuffed Bell Peppers present a vibrant and flavorful culinary experience. These colorful peppers, generously filled with a delightful mixture of cooked quinoa, protein-rich chickpeas, juicy cherry tomatoes, and tangy feta cheese, create a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Infused with aromatic herbs like fresh parsley and oregano, alongside a hint of garlic, these peppers offer a wholesome and nutritious meal. Baked to tender perfection, each bite unveils a symphony of Mediterranean-inspired ingredients, inviting a burst of freshness and savory delight with every mouthful, making them an inviting and satisfying dish for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, parsley, garlic, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stuff each bell pepper with the quinoa mixture and place them in a baking dish.
  4. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until peppers are tender and slightly browned.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with additional parsley if desired.
